About Us

Himakshi makeup & beauty studio

Himakshi Makeup & Beauty Studio is a premier beauty destination nestled in the heart of Bangalipara, Baksa, Assam. We are dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty with expert makeup artistry, skincare, and a full range of salon services. Our studio is a warm, welcoming space where every client is treated like royalty, and every look is crafted with precision and care.

With a perfect 5-star reputation and a passion for staying ahead of beauty trends, our skilled artists and stylists bring creativity and professionalism to every appointment. Whether you're preparing for a wedding, a special occasion, or simply want to pamper yourself, Himakshi Makeup & Beauty Studio is open seven days a week from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM to serve you at your convenience.
